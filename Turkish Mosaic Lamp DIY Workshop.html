2.Something about Turkish Mosaic Lamp

    The word mosaic originates from ancient Greece and is translated from MOSAIC, which originally means a detailed decoration made by mosaic, applied to the wall surface or the floor of the drawing pattern performance of an art.

    Early people living in caves, in order to make the floor more durable, marble paving floor, the earliest mosaic is derived from the development of this basis.

    Ancient Greece when the nobility will be dedicated to the residence, and even tombs for mosaic decoration, to show their noble status. Due to the backward material civilization, the mosaic material at this time is mostly simple black and white tiles, shells, rubble collage patterns, but also figurative life scenes or portraits of people.

    With the progress of the times, mosaic began to construct a myriad of brilliant and colorful patterns, exuding spirituality and mystery, this colorful mosaic art, in Turkey was developed in a big way!
